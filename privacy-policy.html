2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, phone number, and profile information.
  • Purchase Information: When you make a purchase, we collect billing address, shipping address, payment method information, and order history.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us via email, phone, or through our website, we collect the content of your communications, including any attachments.
  • Review and Feedback: When you submit product reviews, ratings, or feedback, we collect this information along with your user profile information.
  • Survey Data: We may collect information through surveys, questionnaires, or market research forms.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit MIOK, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device ID, and mobile network information.
  • Browsing Information: Pages visited, links clicked, time spent on pages, referring URL, and search queries.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather data.
  • IP Address and Location: Your IP address, approximate geographic location, and location derived from IP address.
  • Analytics Data: Information about how you interact with our website collected through analytics tools.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

  • Publicly available sources

  • Customer Service: Responding to your inquiries, resolving disputes, and providing customer support.
  • Communication: Sending transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates, etc.) and marketing communications (with your consent).

3.2 Secondary Uses

  • Website Improvement: Analyzing website usage, improving our services, and developing new features.
  • Marketing and Personalization: Creating targeted advertisements, personalizing your experience, and conducting marketing campaigns.
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent transactions and unauthorized access.
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations.
  • Product Development: Understanding customer preferences and improving our products.

7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to your personal information and obtain a copy of your data in a portable format.

You can update or correct your account information at any time by logging into your account or contacting us directly.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
